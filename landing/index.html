A comprehensive suite of tools built for students and researchers to extract every ounce of knowledge from lectures.

Audio & Video Input

Upload media files or record directly. Supports MP3, WAV, WebM, MP4, MKV, and more.

YouTube Import

Paste a URL and Knowte downloads & transcribes the audio instantly using yt-dlp.

Local Transcription

Speech-to-text using Whisper.cpp with local models from tiny (75MB) to large (3GB).

Structured Notes

LLM-generated notes broken down into topics, key points, examples, and terms.

Interactive Quizzes

Auto-generated MCQs, true/false, and short-answers with explanations and scoring.

Smart Flashcards

Anki-style flashcards with 3-pile study mode and direct exports to .apkg formats.

Mind Maps

Visual tree of concepts via React Flow. Export your mental models to PNG and SVG.

Research Papers

Find related academic papers through Semantic Scholar API (optional cloud feature).

Explain This

Highlight text and get a contextual AI breakdown. Toggle 'simpler' or 'deeper'.

Lecture Library

Persistent SQLite-backed history of all your lectures with blazing fast full-text search.

100% Local & Private

Your data is yours. No cloud APIs, no shadow telemetry. Audio and transcriptions never leave your device.
